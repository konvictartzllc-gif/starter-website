Place your Vosk model folder in this assets directory.

Example folder name:
model-en-us

What Dex needs for the offline wake engine:
1. A Vosk Android model folder inside app/src/main/assets
2. The matching folder name entered in Dex under "Set up wake word"
3. The wake phrase you want Dex to listen for, like "hey dex"

Until those are added, Dex falls back to the basic wake-mode path.
