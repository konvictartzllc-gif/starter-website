# Privacy Policy

Dex AI Assistant collects and uses account data, assistant inputs, optional phone and calendar data, and billing metadata only to deliver the Dex features you enable. Dex does not sell your personal data, does not store full payment card numbers, and is designed to avoid storing highly sensitive information such as passwords, Social Security numbers, or bank details in Dex memory.

Read the full in-app Privacy Policy for details about permissions, data sharing, retention, and deletion requests.
