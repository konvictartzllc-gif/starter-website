# Privacy Policy

**Your Privacy Matters**

- We never store or have access to your credit card numbers. All payments are processed securely by Square; only payment status and transaction IDs are stored.
- Your password is encrypted and never stored in plain text. No one, including admins, can view or retrieve your password.
- Personal information (name, email, preferences) is stored securely and is only used to provide Dex AI services. Admins can see your email and name for account management, but never your password or card info.
- We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.
- You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.
